Our Vision

Restoring Our Forests, Securing Our Future

Sylvan North Forestry is dedicated to acquiring and transforming 100+ acres of land in Northern Ontario into thriving forest ecosystems that benefit both the environment and local communities.

Our integrated approach combines sustainable timber harvesting, tree nursery operations, agroforestry, and carbon sequestration to create a model of regenerative land management.
